Frequently Asked Questions

What happens if the ferry is full?

You may be offered the next available sailing, but this depends on the operator and your ticket type. Flexible tickets give you more options, but availability still matters.

Can I change my ticket on the same day?

Some operators allow last-minute changes with flexible tickets, while others need more notice. Always check the terms at the time of booking.
